What closes the remaining gap
VO₂ Max · +2.0 yrs — 36.7 ml/kg/min is "fair" for your age. AHA optimal for active 29yo male is 43+. Each +1 unit = −0.32 bio years. Zone 4–5 sessions beyond pickleball are the unlock.
HRV · +0.8 yrs — 44.6ms vs 55ms reference. Was 51ms in Jan 2025. Sleep quality, training load, and alcohol all move this. Recoverable quickly.
RHR · +0.9 yrs — 63.6 bpm vs 58 reference. Hit 57.6 bpm during peak pickleball (Aug 2025). Volume is the driver.
Steps · −0.8 yrs — 12,900/day. Top decile for your age. This is your clearest strength in the model. Keep it.
Sleep not in current model: Sleep data only exists from Sep 2024 in this export. Where available, it averages 5.5–6h — below the 7h optimal. Once you have consistent sleep data, adding sleep duration and bedtime SD as drivers would likely push the bio-delta up by ~1.5–2.0 years — meaning the current estimate is probably optimistic. Sleep consistency (WHOOP's primary driver) would be the largest single change to make.
Model — Bio age = chron age + Σ metric deltas. HRV: asymmetric (below 55ms: +0.08/ms; above 55ms: −0.06/ms). RHR: 0.15 yrs/bpm above 58. VO₂Max: 0.32 yrs/unit below 43. Steps: tiered (≥10k=−0.8, ≥8.5k=−0.4, ≥6k=0, ≥4k=+0.8, <4k=+1.8). Sleep where available: <5h=+2.0, 5–6h=+1.2, 6–7h=+0.5, 7–9h=0. 6-month rolling windows. Sources: WHOOP Healthspan White Paper 2025; Nes et al. 2013 HRV all-cause mortality; Paluch et al. 2022 steps; AHA VO₂Max fitness categories. Not a medical assessment.
