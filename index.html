Aug pickleball peak cut the bio-age gap by 2.5 yrs in one month, showing that activity can partially offset the sleep damage.
CeeVaa Nov 2025 – present · 99 nights
Bed 04:27am · Wake 10:14am · Duration 5.5h · SD 2.79h

The step count makes the bio-age number look better than the sleep tells.
